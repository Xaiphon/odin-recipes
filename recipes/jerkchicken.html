Jerk Chicken

Description

  • 1 medium onion, coarsely chopped
  • 3 medium scallions, chopped
  • 2 Scotch bonnet chiles, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon five-spice powder
  • 1 tablespoon allspice berries, coarsely ground
  • 1 tablespoon coarsely ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme, crumbled
  • 1 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 (3 1/2 to 4-pound) chickens, quartered

Steps

  1. In a food processor, combine the onion, scallions, chiles, garlic, five-spice powder, allspice, pepper, thyme, nutmeg, and salt.
  2. Process ingredients to a coarse paste
  3. With the machine on, add the soy sauce and oil in a steady stream
  4. Pour the marinade into a large, shallow dish, add the chicken, and turn to coat
  5. Cover and refrigerate overnight. Bring the chicken to room temperature before proceeding.
  6. Light a grill.
  7. Grill the chicken over a medium-hot fire, turning occasionally, until well browned and cooked through, 35 to 40 minutes.
  8. Transfer the chicken to a platter and serve.
