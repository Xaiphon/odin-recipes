Ackee and Saltfish

Description

  • 2 Canned Ackee
  • 1 lb Saltfish
  • 1 Onion (sliced)
  • 1 Small tomato
  • 1 Sweet bell pepper
  • 1 Stalk scallion
  • 2 Sprig thyme
  • 1/4 Scotch bonnet pepper
  • 1/2 tsp Black pepper
  • 2 tbsp Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Soak the saltfish for a minimum of two hours in cold water.
  2. Pour way the water.
  3. In a saucepan, place the saltfish and cover with fresh water. Put to boil on medium heat for 15 minutes.
  4. Removed from heat and pour away the hot water. Wash the fish in cold water to cool it.
  5. Remove the fish skin and debone the saltfish. Flake the saltfish and set aside.
  6. On medium fire, heat the oil in a cooking skillet. Add the onions, thyme, tomato, sweet pepper, scotch bonnet pepper, scallions and saut√© for 3 minutes.
  7. Add the flaked saltfish and cook for another 3 minutes.
  8. Add the ackee, lower the heat and let it simmer for another 10-15 minutes
  9. Add the black pepper, turn the heat off and serve
